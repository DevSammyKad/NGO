Refund Policy for NGO Counselling Website A refund policy for an NGO

counselling website should clearly specify under what circumstances refunds will be provided, the process for requesting a refund, and any limitations. Here is a sample refund policy:

Eligibility for Refund

Clients are eligible for a refund of fees paid under the following circumstances:


- The client is unable to attend scheduled counselling sessions due to unforeseen circumstances.
- The client finds the counselling services unsuitable or unhelpful after attending initial sessions.
- The NGO is unable to provide the counselling services as promised.

Requesting a Refund

To request a refund, the client should contact the NGO via email or phone within 7 days of missing a scheduled counselling session or deciding the services are unsuitable. The client should provide:


- Client's name and contact information
- Reason for requesting a refund
- Details of the fees paid (payment receipt if available) The NGO will review the request and issue an approval or denial within 5 working days. Approved refunds will be processed within 14 working days via the original payment method.

Non-refundable Fees

The following fees are non-refundable:


- Registration fees
- Fees for counselling sessions already attended

Limitations

The NGO makes no guarantees regarding the outcome or effectiveness of the counselling services provided. Clients must carefully consider if the NGO's services meet their needs before registering and paying fees. That covers the basics of a refund policy for an NGO counselling website. The key elements are:


- Clearly stating the eligibility criteria for refunds
- Providing instructions for how to request a refund
- Specifying any non-refundable fees
