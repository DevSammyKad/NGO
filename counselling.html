Carrer-Counselling

Carrer-Counselling:)

A collaborative process where we enable you to take better decisions related to your education as well as career, which are crucial for your future. We help you understand your strengths and limitations in a better way so you can take informed decisions and remain focused while choosing carrier paths which are right for you. This is achieved by letting you think over a series of questions that revolve around your future plans and career intention. Personal Goals (short/long term) are set to ensure you know what activities you should perform in order to achieve the desired outcome. This helps you understand the progress you make over regular certain time. Some of these goals also include learning skills that will help you achieve new height in your career.

Counselling for Children and Adolescents -

Counselling for Children and Adolescents -:)

This counselling provides a safe and comforting space for the counselees to express their side of the world without any bars. In the due process they learn to explore in a controlled manner and come to terms for expectations vs reality. These sessions use unique techniques like Art Therapy, Talking Therapy, etc. that help you express yourself freely and discreetly. Such sessions definitely prove helpful for adolescents to cope with their mental and physical changes, which are unavoidable for their age group.

Counselling for Stress & Anxiety

Counselling for Stress & Anxiety :)

There are a lot of unpleasant experiences in our lives that leave Stress & Anxiety behind for us to deal with. Stress and Anxiety not only affect your mental health but also negatively impacts people surrounding you. This counseling process assists with issues related to grief, sense of loss, and stress due to various reasons such as loneliness, financial pressures, sadness and anger within personal & professional lives. These counselling sessions are highly recommended for addressing issues related to stress and anxiety in a timely manner to lead a healthy life

Marital Counseling

Marital Counseling:)

Pre-marital counselling sessions help to make sure that you and your spouse can have a strong and healthy relationship throughout your life. Minimal time investment in the form of these sessions helps eliminate a lot to trouble post marriage and avoid mental stress for both the parties involved

post-marital counseling helps a couple to address and voice out their ‘individual’ opinion about the relationship in a safe environment. We ensure you have the confidence to be yourself without the fear of being judged or blamed for a conflict in the marriage. Most of the post-marital problems get solved once you’re able to establish an effective ‘2-Way Communication’ and our counselling sessions help you achieve this goal seamlessly.

Divorce Counseling

Divorce Counseling:)

Sometimes it’s better to part ways in an appropriate manner. There are some relations that are broken irretrievably however the underlying issues are never discussed. This creates a hostile environment for both the parties and leads to further trouble which sometimes is intensely damaging. A counsellor can help distressed couple balance their mental health and make them aware of possible problems in future. Our sessions for such couples help them attain a civil & healthy divorce. Divorce leaves most of the individuals with an emotional turmoil limiting their ability to think reasonably and resolve tangles on their own. Divorce counselling becomes a necessity in such cases. It helps you rationally sail through your emotions and problems to overcome and achieve mental health and social connect

School Counseling

School Counseling:)

Children always have a lot of logical questions relating to their surroundings. It’s important that their queries are addressed in a timely manner. School counseling helps fostering the teacher-student relationship which develops a comfortable environment for children's ability to speak up and ask queries. Every educational institution has its own goals that can be achieved if you have the children's ability to open up and state their opinion. These sessions aim to help the child progress and maintain discipline at the same time.

The activities we have undertaken so far have helped a number of families and individuals in uplifting their standard of living by gaining a better control over their lives which are now stress-free.
