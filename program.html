Program We conduct

Women Empowerment

We promote Women Empowerment to improve women's sense of self-worth, their ability to determine their own choices, and their right to influence social change for themselves and others. There are a number of programs we conduct to uplift their lives, some of these programs include

  • Training programs for Micro-Finance groups for Women
  • Vocational training for women who aspire to work independently and support their families
  • Career Counselling for women to help them identify their strengths they can build upon

Self-Help Group Training Program

Self-Help Groups or SHG's, represents a unique approach to financial intermediator. The approach combines access to low-cost financial services with a process of self-management and development for the women who join as members of an SHG. Various skill training programs for SHG are periodically conducted by Palavi Sanstha to promote leadership qualities & activities generating income for many women through training, improving their abilities and skills. Capacity planning empower to meet the demanding challenges that are required for employment.

  • Palavi's SHG Training: Financial services + self-development for women.
  • Skill Training: Empowers women with income-generating skills.
  • Capacity Building: Prepares women for employment challenges.

Health Education Program

Our health education activities include promoting overall individual health and diseases prevention programs. These programs present information on particular health topics, including health benefits, threats they face and provide tools to build capacity and support. The program empowers individuals and communities to live healthier lives by improving their physical, mental, emotional and social health by increasing their knowledge and influencing their attitudes about caring for their well-being.

Organic Farming Training

Organic farming promotes and enhances agro ecosystem health, including biodiversity biological cycles, & soil biological activity. Organic farming is a technique, which involves the cultivation of plants and rearing of animals in natural ways. This process involves the use of biological materials, avoiding synthetic substances to maintain Soil fertility and ecological balance thereby minimizing pollution & wastage and also produce higher yields than conventional methods. We not only conduct the seminars/awareness sessions for this topic but also support the individuals who need help further information in this important area of farming.
