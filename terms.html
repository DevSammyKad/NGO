Terms and Conditions for Palavi Sanstha Counselling Services

Services Provided

Palavi Sanstha provides the following counselling services:

- Career counselling to help students and job seekers make better education and career decisions. Counselling for children and adolescents to help them cope with mental and physical changes.
- Counselling for stress, anxiety and other mental health issues caused by various reasons.
- Marital counselling for couples, both pre-marital and post-marital.
- Divorce counselling to assist distressed couples going through separation and divorce.
- School counselling to foster a better relationship between students and teachers.

Confidentiality

All information disclosed during counselling sessions is strictly confidential. Client information will not be disclosed to any third party without consent, except as required by law.

Fees

Fees for counselling services are on a sliding scale based on ability to pay. Free or reduced rates are offered to those unable to pay the full fee.

Cancellation Policy

Clients must provide at least 24 hours notice for cancelling an appointment. Clients who miss appointments without proper notice may be charged a cancellation fee.

Termination of Services

Palavi Sanstha may terminate counselling services if: - The client's needs are beyond the scope of services - The client fails to comply with policies - The client behaves in a manner that threatens well-being of staff or other clients

Limitation of Liability

Palavi Sanstha makes no guarantees regarding counselling outcomes and is not liable for damages arising from use of services.

Governing Law
