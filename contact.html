What is Palavi Samajik Sanstha?

Palavi Samajik Sanstha is a private organization operating as a family counseling center in Khed (Rajgurunagar) and nearby areas. Established in 2013, the organization offers various counseling services for families and individuals.

Who is the head of Palavi Samajik Sanstha?

Mrs. Surekha Kad, who holds a Masters in Social Welfare and is a Certified Mental Health and Family Counselor, leads Palavi Samajik Sanstha.

What types of programs does Palavi Samajik Sanstha conduct?

Palavi Samajik Sanstha organizes a range of programs, including Health Awareness Programs, De-Addiction Programs, Career Counseling Seminars, Organic Farming Seminars, Micro-Finance Group Training, Women Empowerment initiatives, and more.

What age groups does Palavi Samajik Sanstha provide counseling for?

Palavi Samajik Sanstha offers counseling for various age groups, including children, adolescents, adults, and couples.

How does Palavi Samajik Sanstha help with family and relationship issues?

Palavi Samajik Sanstha conducts relationship and family counseling sessions to address interpersonal issues, conflicts, and provide strategies for problem-solving and better communication within families.

Is my personal and counseling information kept confidential at Palavi Samajik Sanstha?

Yes, absolutely. At Palavi Samajik Sanstha, we prioritize the confidentiality of all information shared during counseling sessions. Your personal details, discussions, and progress are treated with the utmost respect and kept strictly confidential to ensure your privacy and comfort throughout the counseling process.
